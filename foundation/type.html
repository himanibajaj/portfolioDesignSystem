Typography

Arno Pro and Poppins

The typography used in this design system helps in achieving simplicity and adds life to the elements. Arvo Pro typeface evokes creativity due to its serif characteristics and Poppins is a sans-serifs, it's associated with minimalism because of the simplicity, cleanness, and also being perfect for accessible body text. This typography hierarchy uses the Perfect Fourth Scale with a base font size of 18px.
