Color

The design system uses simple and minimalist color scheme with an inclusion of dark pink that reflects the identity of my portfolio brand. There are a total of six colors used in this system.

White

HEX Code: #FFFFFF

This color is used for the background of the entire portfolio site.


Mouve

HEX Code: #6F69B5

This color is used for button's hover state.


Dark Purple

HEX Code:#5850CF

This color is used for card border, illustrations, and secondary text.


Purple

HEX Code:#6C63FF

This color is used for button's text and background on hover state.


Dark Gray

HEX Code: #3F3D56

This color is used for heading and body text on the page or on the cards.


Gray

HEX Code: #EFEEF0

This color is used in illustrations.


Pink

HEX Code: #FC6681

This color is used in Illustrations.
